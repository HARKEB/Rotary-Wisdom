Mi-34

The Mil Mi-34 is a light, multi-purpose helicopter developed by the Mil Moscow Helicopter Plant, primarily intended for training, light transport, and observation. Designed in the 1980s, the Mi-34 is a compact, agile helicopter with a single piston engine, providing excellent maneuverability and control, making it ideal for pilot training and aerobatics.

Ka-226T

The Kamov Ka-226T is a light, twin-engine helicopter known for its compact, modular design and unique coaxial rotor system, which provides high maneuverability and stability without the need for a tail rotor. Developed in Russia, the Ka-226T is versatile, with interchangeable mission modules that make it ideal for various roles, including medical evacuation, surveillance, and transport.
